The Process

From application to revenue — here's what to expect.

Every client starts with a conversation. Every project ends with measurable results. Here's what happens in between.

1

You apply or request an audit

Takes 3 minutes. Tell me about your brand, your goals, and where copy is letting you down. No CV needed — just honesty about where you are.

2

I review and respond within 48 hours

If it's a free audit, you get a Loom video with specific feedback. If it's an application, we book a strategy call to make sure we're the right fit.

3

We align on scope and start work

I send a brief, we agree on deliverables and timeline, and work begins. No ambiguity. No surprises. Just clear expectations from day one.

4

You receive copy. We measure results.

Every piece is delivered with a strategic rationale. We track open rates, conversion rates, and revenue — then optimise. Your numbers go up or we rewrite it.
