📧 Email🐶 Pet Supplements

Email Revenue · 30 Days

The challenge: A dog supplement brand's email list had 4,200 subscribers but an 18% open rate and near-zero click-through. Revenue from email was negligible.

What I did: Complete rebuild of the 5-email welcome sequence. Subject lines rewritten around owner anxiety and curiosity. Body copy shifted from product features to dog health outcomes. CTAs made specific and benefit-led.

The result: Open rate jumped to 41% in week 1. Email revenue grew 47% month-on-month. The welcome sequence now generates $3,200/month on autopilot.

🛒 Product Pages👶 Baby Food

Revenue Added · 45 Days

The challenge: An organic baby food brand had strong traffic but a 1.2% conversion rate. Their product descriptions listed ingredients but didn't connect emotionally with new parents.

What I did: Rewrote 12 product descriptions to lead with parental peace of mind, ingredient safety, and developmental benefits. Added trust signals and FAQ copy below each product.

The result: Conversion rate rose to 2.8%. Cart abandonment dropped 22%. Revenue increased $14,000 in 45 days without touching ad spend.

📱 Ad Copy🐱 Cat Accessories

ROAS Improvement · 6 Weeks

The challenge: A cat accessories brand was running Facebook ads with feature-heavy copy. ROAS was 1.1 — barely breaking even on spend.

What I did: Rewrote 6 ad variants across 3 angles: owner identity, cat comfort, and unique product story. Headlines led with the cat's experience, not the product's specs.

The result: Best-performing variant hit 4.2× ROAS. Average across all variants: 3.8×. Monthly ad spend stayed the same — revenue from ads nearly quadrupled.

The difference words make.

Example 1 · Dog Supplement Product Description
❌ Before
"Advanced Hip & Joint Formula. Contains glucosamine, chondroitin, and MSM. Recommended by veterinarians. 60 chews per bag. Made in USA."
✓ After
"Watch your dog run like they used to. Our Hip & Joint Formula gives ageing dogs the same mobility support they had in their best years — with vet-trusted ingredients your dog will actually beg for. 60 days of tail wags, guaranteed."
Example 2 · Baby Skincare Email Subject Line
❌ Before (18% open rate)
"New arrivals in our baby skincare range 🌿"
✓ After (41% open rate)
"The one ingredient most baby lotions hide (and why we banned it)"
Example 3 · Facebook Ad Headline
❌ Before (ROAS 1.1×)

✓ After (ROAS 4.2×)
"Your cat ignores every toy you buy. Until now. Built for cats who are bored of boring. (Vet-approved design, 30-day guarantee.)"
